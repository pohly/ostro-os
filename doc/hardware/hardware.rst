Hardware Compatibility
######################

Ostro |trade| OS supports a variety of peripheral interfaces and protocols, including support for common buses like I2C, UART, USB, and more.
This page lists known supported peripherals as of the latest release.  This list is not exhaustive and many peripherals not listed here may be 
compatible as well.
